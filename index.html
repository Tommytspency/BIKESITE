Cyclone Cycles

Who are we?

Cyclone cycles is a Wellington company providing bikes available to hire to the public.
Co-founder Wei Zhang is a mountain bike enthusiast who noticed that there was a lack of
accessible mountain bikes for people wanting to take part in the numerous riding trails around the Wellington region,
without having to transport the bikes from a pickup location to the destined trails. Cyclone Cycles provide mountain bikes and monster scooters on location,
so it is easy to just get on and go! We also provide electric scooters and bikes for transport around the city,
meaning that cars are a completely unnecessary part of your in-city adventure!
